Welcome to the Data Science/AI Primer course for May 2025

This course is for candidates who have already been accepted or applied for any of the data science (with or without AI) programmes offered by the School of Computing and Mathematical Sciences at Birkbeck, except for the undergraduate foundation year, as it is not suitable for that cohort. The course will act as a "jump-start" in your studies.

The module is run in a "blended" style. The cohort reads the materials before class, and most of the face-to-face session is devoted to exercises, questions, and clarification of the material. Apart from the first session, each subsequent session includes homework that consolidates the materials covered and prepares the student for the next session.
